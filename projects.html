2D Fighting Game in Verilog

Developed a Footsies-inspired 2D fighting game with 1-Player and 2-Player modes for the DE1-SOC FPGA developer board. Utilized board features like HEX Displays, buttons, LEDs, GPIO pins, and a VGA output to display game/player states and handle inputs.

Photophone

Designed and constructed a system that transmits audio via light. The circuit takes audio input from a microphone, modulates it onto a LASER LED signal, and transmits it. A receiving circuit with a photodiode and power amplifier demodulates the signal and outputs the audio through a speaker.
